Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Voluntary Data: Name, email, student/employee ID.
  • Automatic Data: Booking timestamps, classroom usage logs, IP address, device type.

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • Facilitate booking management and administrative approvals.
  • Send booking confirmations and reminders.
  • Improve system security and performance.

3. Data Sharing

  • Booking details are visible only to authorized administrators.
  • No third-party sales/sharing without consent, except for legal requirements.
  • Data stored via encrypted cloud services (e.g., AWS, Alibaba Cloud).

4. Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of personal data (contact administrators).
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection (may limit functionality).

5. Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers.
  • Regular security audits and access controls.
  • Administrators sign confidentiality agreements.

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use session cookies to maintain login status.
  • Disable cookies via browser settings (may affect functionality).

7. Policy Updates

Changes will be notified via email or system announcements. Continued use implies acceptance.
